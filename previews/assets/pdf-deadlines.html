Important Notes
  • If a deadline falls on a weekend or federal holiday, it moves to the next business day.
  • Extensions grant extra time to file — not extra time to pay. Taxes owed are still due by the original deadline.
  • Georgia state deadlines generally match federal individual deadlines. Alabama may differ — ask us.
  • Dates above reflect standard federal rules. Always confirm with your CPA for your specific situation.
