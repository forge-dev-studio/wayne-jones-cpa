Small-Business
Tax-Prep Checklist
What to gather before meeting with your CPA — Tax Year 2025

What Everyone Should Bring

Income Documents

  • All W-2s (wages from every employer)
  • 1099-NEC / 1099-MISC (freelance / contract income)
  • 1099-INT (bank interest)
  • 1099-DIV (dividends)
  • 1099-B / brokerage year-end statement (investments)
  • 1099-SSA (Social Security benefits, if applicable)
  • K-1 forms (from partnerships, S-corps, trusts)
  • Prior-year tax return (federal + state)

Deductions & Credits

  • Mortgage interest statement (Form 1098)
  • Property tax bills paid in 2025
  • State & local tax paid (if itemizing)
  • Charitable contribution receipts / letters
  • Healthcare / HSA / FSA info (Form 1095-A/B/C)
  • Childcare provider name, address, EIN & amount paid
  • Education expenses / 1098-T (tuition statement)
  • Estimated tax payments made in 2025 (dates + amounts)

Entity-Specific Documents

Sole Proprietor / Single-Member LLC (Schedule C)
  • Gross revenue / sales records
  • Bank & credit card statements (business accounts)
  • All business expense receipts by category
  • Home-office measurements (sq ft office / home total) if claiming home-office deduction
  • Mileage log — business miles driven in 2025 date, destination, purpose
  • Inventory records (beginning + ending count + cost)
  • Equipment/asset purchases (date, description, cost)
  • Any assets sold or disposed of
  • Self-employed health insurance premiums paid
  • SEP-IRA / SIMPLE / Solo 401(k) contributions
S-Corporation (Form 1120-S + personal 1040)
  • Profit & loss statement (full year)
  • Balance sheet (beginning & end of year)
  • Payroll records — officer W-2 wages paid
  • Shareholder loan balances (beginning + ending)
  • Distributions paid to shareholders
  • Copies of all 941s filed (quarterly payroll returns)
  • Health insurance premiums paid for >2% shareholders
  • Prior-year basis schedule (if available)
  • Any new assets or equipment purchased
  • State filing confirmation / certificate of good standing
Partnership / Multi-Member LLC (Form 1065)
  • Profit & loss statement & balance sheet
  • Capital account balances for each partner
  • Partner distributions paid in 2025
  • Guaranteed payments to partners
  • Partnership agreement (if changed in 2025)
  • New asset purchases and disposals
  • Any debt changes (new loans, payoffs)
  • Prior-year 1065 + all K-1s issued

Payroll (If You Have Employees)

  • Total wages paid to all employees (by employee)
  • Copies of all W-2s issued for 2025
  • Copies of 941s (quarterly) & 940 (annual FUTA) filed
  • 1099-NEC issued to independent contractors (name, address, SSN/EIN, amount)
  • Health insurance / benefits premiums paid on behalf of employees
  • Retirement plan contributions (employer match, SIMPLE, SEP contributions)
Before Your Appointment The more organized your documents, the faster and more accurate your return — and the less it costs. Scan or photograph receipts as you go throughout the year. If you're missing a document, request a duplicate from the issuer before your appointment. Not sure if something applies to you? Bring it anyway and ask us.
