## What Business Consulting Includes

Our consulting work covers the areas where small business owners most often need a trusted advisor:

**Entity selection and structure.** Deciding between a sole proprietorship, LLC, or S-corp is one of the most consequential choices you'll make early in a business. We walk through the general tax and financial implications of each option so you can make an informed decision — in coordination with your attorney for the legal side.

**New-business setup.** Getting the financial side of a new business set up correctly from day one makes everything easier: cleaner books, clearer tax obligations, and fewer surprises. We help new business owners understand what they need to have in place before they open the doors.

**Cash-flow and budgeting guidance.** Profitability on paper doesn't always mean money in the bank. We help business owners understand the difference, anticipate lean periods, and build habits that keep cash moving predictably through the business.

**Growth and profitability advice.** When revenue is climbing but profits aren't keeping pace — or when you're considering a significant investment in equipment, staff, or a second location — having a CPA look at the numbers first can clarify whether the move makes financial sense.

**Tax-smart business decisions.** Owner compensation, retirement contributions, timing of major purchases — decisions like these interact with your tax situation in ways that aren't always intuitive. We help you think through the financial implications before you act.
